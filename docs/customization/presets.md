# Industry Presets

The eShopping theme includes **4 industry presets**. Each preset customizes the color palette, placeholder text, trust badges, and messaging to match a specific industry --- so you can launch a polished, on-brand store without tweaking dozens of settings by hand.

---

## How to Apply a Preset

1. Go to **Storefront > My Themes**.
2. Click **Customize** on the eShopping theme.
3. In **Theme Styles**, select the preset variation from the style selector at the top of the panel.

## Industrial (Default)

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Primary accent** | Terra / burnt orange (`#bf5b33`) |
| **Neutral scale** | Warm bark tones |
| **Trust strip** | "Made in USA", "Free Shipping", "4.8 Star Rating", "Expert Support" |
| **Search phrases** | "Search for power tools...", "Find welding equipment..." |
| **Cart goals** | $50 Free Shipping | $100 10% Off | $150 Free Gift |

**Best for:** Hardware, tools, welding, construction, industrial equipment.

## Preset Comparison

| Feature | Industrial | AutoParts | Packaging | Electronics |
|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| **Accent color** | Terra `#bf5b33` | Amber `#d97706` | Emerald `#059669` | Blue `#3b82f6` |
| **Neutrals** | Bark (warm) | Slate (cool) | Stone (neutral) | Zinc (cool) |
| **Free shipping at** | $50 | $50 | $75 | $30 |
| **Discount at** | $100 (10%) | $100 (10%) | $150 (10%) | $75 (5%) |
| **Bonus at** | $150 (Gift) | $200 (Gift) | $300 (Samples) | $150 (Accessory) |

!!! tip "Presets are a starting point"
    After applying a preset you can still override any individual setting --- colors, trust messages, cart goals, search phrases --- through the Theme Editor. The preset simply sets sensible defaults for your industry.
