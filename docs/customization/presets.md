# Industry Presets

The eShopping theme comes with **4 ready-made presets** designed for different industries. Each preset gives your store an instant professional look by adjusting colors, trust messaging, search suggestions, and cart incentive goals --- so you can get up and running without customizing dozens of settings one by one.

---

## How to Switch Presets

1. Go to **Storefront** > **My Themes**.
2. Click **Customize** on the eShopping theme.
3. At the top of the **Theme Styles** panel, open the style selector dropdown.
4. Choose one of the four presets: **Industrial**, **AutoParts**, **Packaging**, or **Electronics**.
5. Click **Save** to apply.

<div class="placeholder-screenshot">Theme Editor style selector showing the four preset variations: Industrial, AutoParts, Packaging, Electronics</div>

!!! tip "Presets are just a starting point"
    After applying a preset, you can still fine-tune any individual setting --- colors, messages, cart goals, search suggestions --- through the Theme Editor. The preset simply gives you a polished set of defaults for your industry.

## Industrial (Default)

The default preset uses **warm, earthy tones** --- a rich burnt orange accent paired with warm brown and tan neutrals. It feels rugged, trustworthy, and professional.

**Trust strip messages:** "Made in USA" -- "Free Shipping" -- "4.8 Star Rating" -- "Expert Support"

**Search suggestions:** Power tools, welding equipment, and similar industrial products

**Cart incentive goals:**

- Spend $50 --- Free Shipping
- Spend $100 --- 10% Off
- Spend $150 --- Free Gift

**Best for:** Hardware stores, tool suppliers, welding shops, construction supply, industrial equipment dealers.
