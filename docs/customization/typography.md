# Customizing Typography

The eShopping theme uses a carefully chosen set of three fonts that work together to give your store a polished, professional look. You can change these fonts through the Theme Editor without any coding.

---

## The Three Font Families

### Headings --- Playfair Display (Serif)

Your store's headings --- page titles, section headings, and hero banners --- use **Playfair Display**, an elegant serif typeface. It gives headings a refined, editorial quality that stands out from the body text.

**Where you will see it:** Page titles, section headers, promotional banners, and any text styled as a heading.

**How to change it:** Go to **Theme Styles** > **General** > **Typography** and look for the **Headings Font** setting.

### Body Text --- Source Sans 3 (Sans-Serif)

All general text on your store --- product descriptions, navigation links, buttons, form labels, and everything else --- uses **Source Sans 3**, a clean and highly readable sans-serif typeface.

**Where you will see it:** Product descriptions, navigation menus, buttons, form fields, cart details, and all other general text.

**How to change it:** Go to **Theme Styles** > **General** > **Typography** and look for the **Body Font** setting.

---

### Prices and Codes --- IBM Plex Mono (Monospace)

For data-oriented content like prices, SKU numbers, product codes, and quantities, the theme uses **IBM Plex Mono** --- a monospace (fixed-width) font. This makes numbers and codes easy to scan and gives them a distinct, technical look that separates them from regular text.

**Where you will see it:** Product prices, SKU numbers, product codes, quantity fields, and other numerical or technical values.

**How to change it:** Go to **Theme Styles** > **eShopping** > **Fonts** and look for the **Monospace Font** dropdown. You can choose from several monospace options including IBM Plex Mono, IBM Plex Mono Medium, and JetBrains Mono.

## How Fonts Work Together

The three font families each serve a distinct purpose, creating a clear visual hierarchy:

| Role | Font Style | Where It Appears |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Headings** | Serif (elegant, editorial) | Page titles, section headers, banners |
| **Body text** | Sans-serif (clean, readable) | Descriptions, menus, buttons, forms |
| **Prices and codes** | Monospace (technical, scannable) | Prices, SKUs, product codes, quantities |

This combination helps customers navigate your store intuitively --- headings catch attention, body text is easy to read, and prices and codes stand out clearly.

---

## Balanced Size Scale

The theme includes a carefully designed text size scale that ensures everything looks proportional --- from tiny badge labels up to large hero text. All sizes are balanced relative to each other, so when you see small helper text next to a section heading, the proportions always feel right.

You do not need to adjust individual text sizes. The theme handles this automatically to maintain a clean, consistent look across every page of your store.

---

## Tips for Choosing Fonts

!!! tip "Stick with the defaults or choose complementary pairs"
    The default font combination (Playfair Display + Source Sans 3 + IBM Plex Mono) is professionally curated and works well for most stores. If you do change fonts, keep these guidelines in mind:

    - **Headings:** Serif fonts (like Playfair Display) add elegance, while sans-serif fonts (like Montserrat) feel more modern and approachable.
    - **Body text:** Always choose a highly readable sans-serif font. Avoid decorative or script fonts for body text --- they are hard to read at small sizes.
    - **Monospace:** This font is used for prices and codes, so legibility is key. The available options in the Theme Editor are all excellent choices.

!!! tip "Preview before saving"
    After changing a font in the Theme Editor, the live preview on the right side of the screen updates immediately. Browse several pages (home, category, product) to make sure the new font looks good everywhere before clicking **Save**.
