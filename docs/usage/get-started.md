# Get Started

Thank you for using our theme!

To set up the theme exactly like our demo stores, you may need to install our free app **[PapaThemes Widgets](https://www.bigcommerce.com/apps/papathemes-widgets/)**. This app provides additional widgets to visually build content in Page Builder without requiring coding skills.

## Customizing Typography, Fonts, and Colors

Customize typography, fonts, colors, and buttons in **Page Builder** > **Theme Styles** > **General**. There are dozens of options to help you beautifully and uniquely customize your website.

<div class="placeholder-screenshot">Page Builder > Theme Styles > General panel showing typography, font, and color options</div>

## Customizing the Checkout Page's Typography and Colors

Customize the checkout page's typography and colors in **Page Builder** > **Theme Styles** > **Optimized Checkout**.

<div class="placeholder-screenshot">Page Builder > Theme Styles > Optimized Checkout panel</div>

## Installing the PapaThemes Widgets App

Find and install the **[PapaThemes Widgets](https://www.bigcommerce.com/apps/papathemes-widgets/)** app from the [BigCommerce Apps Marketplace](https://www.bigcommerce.com/apps/).

After installation, navigate to your admin panel. In the left sidebar, click **Apps** > **My Apps**. Then, click the **Launch** button for the PapaThemes Widgets app to open it:

<div class="placeholder-screenshot">BigCommerce admin panel showing Apps > My Apps with PapaThemes Widgets Launch button</div>

Next, click the **Install** buttons in sequence to install all the available widgets.

<div class="placeholder-screenshot">PapaThemes Widgets app showing Install buttons for all available widgets</div>

Go to **Storefront** > **My Themes**. Click **Customize** on the eShopping theme's dropdown menu to open Page Builder. Select any theme style to begin.

<div class="placeholder-screenshot">My Themes page with Customize button on eShopping theme dropdown</div>

Confirm the widgets are installed by verifying they appear in Page Builder.

<div class="placeholder-screenshot">Page Builder sidebar showing PapaThemes custom widgets available</div>

## eShopping Settings in Theme Editor

The eShopping theme includes a dedicated **eShopping** section in the Theme Editor that contains all eShopping-specific settings. To access these settings:

1. Go to **Storefront** > **My Themes**
2. Click **Customize** on the eShopping theme
3. In the left panel, scroll down to find the **eShopping** section

This section provides over 85 configurable options organized into groups covering layout, colors, typography, header, sidebar, product pages, cart, search, marketing features, and more. All settings use the `eshopping-` prefix and are unique to this theme.

<div class="placeholder-screenshot">Theme Editor left panel showing the eShopping section expanded with setting groups</div>

## Selecting a Preset

The eShopping theme ships with **four industry presets** designed to give you a head start for different types of stores. Each preset customizes the theme's colors, typography, placeholder text, and industry-specific content so your store looks polished right away.

To select a preset:

1. Go to **Storefront** > **My Themes**
2. Click **Customize** on the eShopping theme
3. In the left panel, navigate to **Theme Styles**
4. Choose one of the available presets

### Industrial

The Industrial preset uses a bold, utilitarian color palette with dark tones and strong accents. It is suited for stores selling tools, hardware, safety equipment, and industrial supplies.

<div class="placeholder-screenshot">eShopping storefront with Industrial preset applied -- dark header, bold accent colors, industrial product imagery</div>

### AutoParts

The AutoParts preset uses a high-contrast color scheme with automotive-inspired accents. It is designed for stores selling auto parts, accessories, and vehicle maintenance products.

<div class="placeholder-screenshot">eShopping storefront with AutoParts preset applied -- automotive color scheme, parts-oriented product layout</div>

### Packaging

The Packaging preset uses clean, professional tones with neutral colors. It is tailored for stores selling packaging supplies, shipping materials, and bulk packaging products.

<div class="placeholder-screenshot">eShopping storefront with Packaging preset applied -- clean neutral tones, packaging product imagery</div>

### Electronics

The Electronics preset uses a modern, tech-forward color palette with cool tones and sharp accents. It is built for stores selling electronics, gadgets, components, and tech accessories.

<div class="placeholder-screenshot">eShopping storefront with Electronics preset applied -- modern tech color scheme, electronics product layout</div>

After selecting a preset, you can further customize any individual setting in the **eShopping** section to fine-tune the look and feel for your brand.
